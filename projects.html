NEW BUILD:

 

  • Gala Supermarket, Co. Westmeath. Mixed use development comprising of the construction of a two storey building, with bottom floor for retail use and upper split into two separate apartments. Our works included all site clearance, construction, installation of services and groundworks, including laying a new 4” water main, large car parking area, surface water drainage and an effluent treatment plant.

 

  • Women’s Community Projects, Mullingar, Co. Westmeath. Two storey crèche building. Demolition of existing buildings at rear of Mullingar Community Centre. Construction, fit-out and groundworks for new crèche building. Works included all M&E services required in a modern building, a 3m high reinforced concrete retaining wall and installation of a playground facility.

 

  • Autistic Care Unit, Milltownpass National School, Milltownpass, Co. Westmeath. We were awarded the contract to construct this additional wing at the school. Works included all construction, services & fit-out, including reinforced concrete frame and groundworks. A consideration here was tying the new building into the existing structure and operating to a restricted programme during Summer holidays to facilitate normal school operation.

 

  • Residential Projects, Midlands. Numerous architect-designed high specification residential projects for private clients. We carried out all site clearance, construction, services installation, fit-out, groundworks and handover procedures. Buildings incorporate latest in renewable energy sources and energy efficient materials.

 

 

RESTORATION:

 

  • Three Storey Building in Town Center, Mullingar, Co. Westmeath. We demolished existing partitions and fixtures, followed by fit-out to become a modern retail outlet over two storeys with storage on top floor, for a private client.

 

  • Restoration of an Edwardian country residence to meet today's living demands while retaining the period character of the house.

 

  • Restoration of a traditional Irish farmhouse and incorporation into a modern, high-spec dwelling.
